Pad Rater


Price

  1) What is your budget?
  2) What is the rent per person?
  3) Utilities per person?
  4) Cost of insurance for each person?

Location

  1) How far will your commute be?
  2) How will you travel?
  3) Which of these is within walking distance? (Hold shift to select multiple)

Size

  1) How many people are you with?
  2) How many bedrooms?
  3) How many bathrooms?
  4) Square footage?

Landlord

  1) Was it easy to contact the landlord?
  2) Is the landlord willing to negotiate terms of lease?
  3) Will the landlord put commitments to you in writing?

Lease Terms

  1) Will you have to pay any of the followings? (Hold shift to select multiple)
  2) Are utilities included?
  3) Will you be staying the full term of the lease?
  4) Will the term of the lease be extended automatically?
  5) Does the rent have late fees?

Misc

  1) Are the following in suitable working condition? (Hold shift to select multiple)
  2) Will the landlord walk through the property to note repairs needed?
